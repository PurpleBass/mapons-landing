Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mapons ("App") handles your information.

1. Data We Process

Mapons may process:

  • Data you import (for example CSV rows, addresses, GeoJSON, map metadata)
  • Location data (if you grant permission) for map-centering and location-based actions
  • Exported map outputs you choose to create
  • Account and backup metadata when you configure optional cloud backup
  • AI prompt content you submit in the Analyst feature

2. On-Device Storage

By default, your map data and app state are stored locally on your device using on-device app storage.

3. Optional Cloud Backup (Supabase)

If you enable cloud backup:

  • You provide your Supabase project URL and anon key in the app.
  • You may sign in (magic link) to associate backups with your account.
  • Backup payloads are uploaded to your configured Supabase project.
  • You can restore or delete backups from within the app.

Cloud backup is optional and can be disabled at any time.

4. AI Analyst Feature

If you use the Analyst feature and configure an AI provider/API key:

  • Prompts and relevant map context may be sent to the selected AI provider to generate responses.
  • Data handling by that provider is governed by the provider's own terms and privacy policy.
  • You control whether to enable this feature and what content you submit.

5. Permissions

Mapons may request:

  • Location permission: to show your position, center the map, or drop a location-based pin.
  • Photos permission (add-only): to save exported images to your photo library.

You can manage permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only as needed to provide features you enable (for example Supabase backup or AI provider requests).

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local data remains on-device until you delete it in the app or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud backups remain in your configured Supabase project until deleted.
  • You can remove cloud credentials/sign out from the app.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical steps to protect data, but no method of storage or transmission is guaranteed to be fully secure.

9. Children's Privacy

Mapons is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Use

If you use cloud/AI features, data may be processed in countries other than your own depending on your selected providers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date reflects the latest version.
